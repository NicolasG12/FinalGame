You're Gary, a student at College University, studying late at night.
But wait! Someone left the library's Necronomicon open, releasing shadow demons into the school.
While rampaging throughout the school, the demons rip the ritual pages from the Necronomicon and hide them throughout the school.
Collect the missing pages and return them to the Necronomicon altar to send the demons back to Hell.

CONTROLS

Move Gary using the arrow keys.
Go to the left to enter the Science Lab.
Find the missing page and return it to the altar in the starting room (colorful tile).
Be careful of the demons patroling the area.
